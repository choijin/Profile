This Tableau project analyzes crime incidents occurring at New York City parks and supports exploration by county and park.

- Preprocessed data after importing a PDF data file.
- Combined PDF data with spatial files using an inner join on park names.
- Designed a visualization to help motivate safer park planning and police patrol decisions.
