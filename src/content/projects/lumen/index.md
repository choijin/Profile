---
title: "Lumen"
description: "A tiny CLI that turns your shell history into a personal knowledge base."
date: "2026-02-21"
repoURL: "https://github.com"
---

Lumen explores the idea that your command history is a rough draft of your technical memory. It groups repeated commands, extracts useful patterns, and lets you annotate the ones you want to remember.

I built it as a small local-first tool because this kind of data should stay close to the person who created it.
