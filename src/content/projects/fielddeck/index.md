FieldDeck is a focused labeling interface for small datasets. It is designed for the moment before a team needs a heavy annotation platform, when speed and clarity matter more than enterprise workflow features.

The app keeps the labeling loop tight: keyboard shortcuts, clear progress, and quick export formats for common machine learning pipelines.
